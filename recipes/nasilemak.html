Nasi Lemak

Introduction

Nasi Lemak is a Malaysian word, and it translates to English as "Fat Rice". If I were to actually break down the dish to it's core, it is basically rice with a side serve of chilli. The devil is in the details, however, and the rice is cooked with coconut cream/fat and the chilli paste is packed with flavour! Let's dig in.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Coconut Milk
  • Sambal Paste
  • Onions
  • Anchovies
  • Cucumber

Steps

  1. Wash the rice.
  2. Measure the milk thing and add it to the rice.
  3. Place the rice on heat and make it cook to perfection.
  4. Cut the onion to size and prep the ingredients by cutting to size.
  5. Fry onion until aromatic and soft, then add the sambal paste and fry to incorporate the flavours.
  6. In a separate pot, put water in to boil, add the eggs (in it's shell) and cook until it's how you like it.
  7. Once ready, assemble all on the plate and serve!
