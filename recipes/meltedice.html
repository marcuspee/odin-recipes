Melted Ice

Introduction

Melted Ice is a construct. You might not know how to make it, but the complexity of this dish can be broken down into a few key steps.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Water
  • Ice
  • Steam

Steps

  1. Measure out the correct amount of H2O.
  2. Superheat a container and quickly dash in the measured amount of liquid water.
  3. Trap the vapours with your best vacuum cleaner and ensure the condensation is gathered appropriately.
  4. Successfully achieving that, it get's easier from here. Contain all remaining condensation into a ice making tray and freeze to oblivion.
  5. When ready, remove from freezer and take a burning torch to it for approximately 15 seconds to quickly melt the product.
  6. Serve warm and quick.
